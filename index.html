1.3.4. Concept of an Analyzed Frame

A frame can be analyzed in multiple ways:

  1. detected faces in frame matched against enrollments.
  2. detected faces in frame matched against a loaded image (may or may not be known to FR).
    • how close of a match is the detected image to the provided image.
  3. 2 images compared with each other.

1.4. Ray’s notes

The following are system requirements from the company Clearview AI. The ones offering their service to PDs using images harvested off of all social media sites like Facebook & Instagram.

The Capture fields are very import when it comes time for the Police to justify who they were searching for and why. Since we are offering an on-premise appliance, I am not as concerned about the Criminal Justice Information System security requirements. If the FR appliance is behind their firewall, it is on them.

we will be asked if the app has been penetration tested and we’ll have to address this down the road.

Note – It is my understanding the FR app from Clearview AI only can process still images, no video streams. This is our competitive advantage.
